3-month performance insights
Revenue growing consistently
Attributed revenue has grown every month: $18.5K → $33.8K → $50.4K. That's a 172% increase from January to March in just 3 months. The retargeting flywheel is compounding — as the lead pool grows, RT converts more efficiently each month.
Followers campaign: major leap in March
Cost/follower dropped from $0.70 (Feb) to $0.27 (Mar), entering the "Great" benchmark. IG bio leads 5×'d from 204 to 1,101 in one month. This shows creative improvement — March's content is significantly stronger at capturing high-intent audience.
CPL creeping up — watch in April
New Leads CPL improved from $0.77 (Jan) to $0.70 (Feb) then rose back to $0.91 (Mar) — above the $0.80 "decent" threshold. Creative fatigue is likely. Recommend testing fresh ad creative in April to push CPL back below $0.80 before it trends higher.
